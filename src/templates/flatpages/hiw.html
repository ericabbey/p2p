How it works

This is how the platform is achitechtured to get you near your goal

Level 1: pay your referer 0.002 Btc

Get 3 X 0.002 Btc = 0.006Btc - 0.002 Btc = 0.004 Btc profit

Level 2: pay your level 2 upliner 0.003 Btc

Get 9 X 0.003 Btc = 0.027Btc - 0.003 Btc = 0.024 Btc profit

Level 3: pay your level 3 upliner 0.02 Btc

Get 27 X 0.02 Btc = 0.54Btc - 0.02 Btc = 0.52 Btc profit

Level 4: pay your level 4 upliner 0.45 Btc

Get 81 X 0.45 Btc = 36.45Btc

No more upgrades after level 4

You earn a profit of 36.45Btc. All money made goes to your bitcoin wallet. You can check the worth of your bitcoin from Google or BtcCalculator

You can start a new cycle every 180 days
